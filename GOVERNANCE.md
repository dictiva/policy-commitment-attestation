# Governance

## Stewardship

This repository is currently stewarded by [Dictiva](https://dictiva.com), pending a project proposal to the Linux Foundation [Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF)](https://aaif.io/).

## Decision making (v0.1)

- **Proposals** — filed as GitHub issues labelled `proposal` in this repository.
- **Review window** — minimum 14 calendar days from open, to allow asynchronous review across time zones.
- **Acceptance** — by consensus among Dictiva maintainers. On explicit disagreement, a two-thirds majority of active maintainers is required to overrule an objection.
- **Rejection** — documented in the closing comment with rationale.

## Transition to AAIF

On acceptance of a PCA project proposal by the AAIF Technical Steering Committee (see Dictiva ADR-040 §4 and Epic E #2305), governance transitions to the AAIF-standard process:

- Project lifecycle: Growth → Impact → Emeritus
- TSC-mediated decision making
- Community-elected maintainer roster
- Apache License 2.0 retained

At that point, this `GOVERNANCE.md` will be superseded by the AAIF-standard governance template.

## Conflict of interest

Maintainers with a commercial stake in a specific outcome (for example, a competing implementation) MUST recuse themselves from the decision record on that proposal. Recusal is noted in the issue.
